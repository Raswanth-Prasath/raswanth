Bio

I am a Civil Engineering student at Arizona State University, specializing in transportation engineering with a focus on traffic modeling, geospatial analysis, and data-driven infrastructure solutions. My work involves developing intelligent transportation systems, conducting traffic impact studies, and leveraging machine learning and GIS tools to improve urban mobility and safety.

My professional experience includes designing traffic control systems and pedestrian signals at HR Green, conducting advanced GIS analysis at the School of Earth and Space Exploration, and performing traffic engineering consulting at Concept Dash. I completed my B.Tech. in Civil Engineering at NIT Tiruchirappalli, where I developed expertise in traffic calming measures and road safety at the Centre of Excellence for Road Safety (CoERS), IIT Madras. I am passionate about combining Python, QGIS, traffic simulation tools (VISSIM, Synchro), and machine learning to solve complex transportation challenges and contribute to safer, more efficient urban systems.
